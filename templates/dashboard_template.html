CBT Practice Platform

📖 How to Use This Platform

This CBT (Computer Based Test) interface is designed to help aspirants practice Previous Year Questions (PYQs) and track their attempt statistics.

  1. Download the Question Paper: Due to limitations in displaying complex mathematical equations and formatting, this interface should be used alongside another device displaying the actual question paper. Please download the respective PDF from the All_test_papers_pdf folder in the repository beforehand.
  2. Attempt the Exam: Use this interface to mark your answers and manage your time just like the real CBT exam (Note: ISI exams use a pen-and-paper OMR format, but you can still use this tool to record your attempts).
  3. Download Your Report: After submitting the exam, click the button to download a CSV report containing your marked options, time taken per question, and other statistics.
  4. Calculate Your Result: Open the compiled Excel sheet provided in the repository, and copy-paste your attempts from the CSV to calculate your final score.
